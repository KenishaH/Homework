Welcome to the Playground

Rules to follow

Adults who supervise children's use of playground equipment can help prevent injuries. If a child is hurt, an adult can assist them and provide any immediate first aid that may be required. On the playground, children should always be supervised by an adult. Watch out for small children, as they may not be able to judge distance or anticipate risky circumstances. On the playground, older children often push themselves, so it's critical that an adult supervises them.

  • Be mindful of surroundings
  • Look before using
  • be kind
  • tie your shoes

Follow the following rules if in danger

  1. For any safety concerns call 911
  2. Try to remain calm
  3. Find safety
